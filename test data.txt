Spam examples:

"Congratulations! You've won a free vacation to the Bahamas! Reply now to claim your prize."
"Get a loan now! No credit check required. Fast approval guaranteed."
"You have been selected for a special offer. Click here to claim your reward."
"URGENT: Your bank account has been compromised. Please update your information immediately."
"Limited time offer: 50% off all purchases! Don't miss out on this amazing deal."

"Hi, just checking in to see how you're doing. Let's catch up soon!"
"Reminder: Your appointment is scheduled for tomorrow at 10:00 AM. See you there!"
"Thanks for your order! Your package has been shipped and will arrive by Friday."
"Hey, do you want to grab lunch later? There's a new restaurant I want to try."
"Congratulations on your promotion! Let's celebrate tonight."
